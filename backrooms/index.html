The Backrooms

Play The Backrooms Game Unblocked

The Backrooms is an online game inspired by a popular internet phenomenon. Players find themselves trapped in an eerie, seemingly endless maze of empty yellow rooms. This unsettling environment, known as a "liminal space," originated from a 2019 4chan post and has since captured the imagination of many.

The game challenges players to navigate through this surreal, expansive setting while avoiding mysterious entities. With its unique concept of "no-clipping out of reality," The Backrooms offers an immersive and unsettling experience that blends elements of exploration, survival, and psychological horror.
